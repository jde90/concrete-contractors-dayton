Where We Work

Concrete Contractor Service Areas in the Dayton Metro

We pour driveways, patios, sidewalks, stamped concrete, foundations, and more throughout Montgomery, Greene, and Warren counties. Click your city below for local details, pricing notes, and project examples.

Kettering

South of Dayton · Montgomery County. Mature 1950s-70s neighborhoods with aging driveways and patios ready for replacement. One of our busiest service areas.

Centerville

South of Dayton · Washington Township. Upscale residential with well-maintained homes. Popular for stamped concrete, decorative patios, and curb-appeal upgrades.

Beavercreek

East of Dayton · Greene County. Near Wright-Patterson AFB. Mix of newer construction and established neighborhoods. Strong demand from military families.

Huber Heights

North of Dayton · Montgomery County. One of the largest planned communities in the U.S. Many original 1960s-70s driveways and sidewalks need replacement.

Fairborn

East of Dayton · Greene County. Home to Wright-Patterson AFB and Wright State University. Residential and light commercial concrete needs.

Miamisburg

South of Dayton · Montgomery County. Along the Great Miami River with a historic downtown. Older concrete meets newer subdivisions. River-valley soil conditions.

Springboro

South of Dayton · Warren County. Fast-growing suburb with new construction foundations, driveways, and patios going in every month.

Xenia

East of Dayton · Greene County seat. Tornado-rebuilt areas alongside historic neighborhoods. Mix of old and new construction concrete needs.
